Hidar Sitaten,Lebu

by Saron on January 29 ,2020

Hidar Sitaten, Smoked Hidar, the Ethiopian version of sprig cleaning, when household rubbish and refuse are gathered and set alight each year on hidar 12 of Ethiopian calander, or Nov 21. Hidar sitaten, is a custom that originates in a distant past, a century ago to be exact, when the deadly second wave of spanish Flu pandemic arrived in Ethiopia and causes devastations and ruins.
